Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

Our first priority is listening — understanding where our clients need systems that boost team productivity and reduce risk. We design scalable solutions for owners and contractors, offering services like project setup, technical systems development, and support for industry tools such as Procore, PlanGrid, and Autodesk Build.

How do you help teams start projects?

We define project scope, align stakeholders, and develop clear roadmaps. This approach ensures team clarity on responsibilities, timelines, and deliverables.

Do you provide support with technical systems and documentation?

Yes. We create implementation tools, workflows, and documentation systems, including custom checklists, platform setup, and integrated permitting and construction documentation.

Can you help during an active project?

We offer focused support for ongoing projects, such as schedule reviews, space plan validation, and consultant coordination to maintain project momentum.

What is AI integration?

AI integration means deploying artificial intelligence into your existing workflows — not replacing your team, but making them faster and more effective. We implement person by person, workflow by workflow, with enterprise security from day one.

How long does AI implementation take?

6 weeks from kickoff to operational. Week 0 is executive intake, weeks 1–4 cover champion training and workforce intake, weeks 5–6 are training and pattern library handoff. Ongoing optimization continues monthly.

What does AI integration cost?

Intake and setup are flat fee — you know the cost before we start. Ongoing optimization is time and materials. No retainers, no minimums, no long-term contracts unless you want one.

Do I need technical staff for AI integration?

No. We select and train internal champions from your existing team. The champion role is about understanding workflows and driving adoption, not writing code.

We're happy to talk through your specific situation — no obligation.
